About Genki English

GenkiEnglish.com is a registered company located in Ehime, Japan, serving schools and teachers in over 190 countries. Richard Graham is the lead educational consultant for Genki English and is the world's leading authority on teaching in a fun, engaging and 'genki' way.

That's right, "Genki" means "fun", "exciting" and "full of life" in Japanese. Genki English's vision is to make learning English fun and effective. Remember, when children are having fun, they are more motivated to learn. They are more open to new ideas and more willing to participate. Therefore, our mission is to create an exciting learning environment where children can enjoy the process of learning English.
